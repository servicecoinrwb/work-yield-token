WorkYieldTokenV2

This smart contract tokenizes future yield from work orders using pUSD as a stablecoin.

Overview

  • ERC20 Token: WYT
  • Stablecoin: pUSD
  • Features: Mint from work orders, redeem with fee, reserve handling
